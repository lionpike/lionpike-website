What we do

Your ERP knows more than you think.

Most manufacturing facilities are sitting on a goldmine of operational data. The problem isn’t the data — it’s the work required to get to it. Across decades of experience on the factory floor, we’ve seen the same pattern repeat: someone opens the ERP, pulls a table, pastes it into Excel, and spends two hours producing a report that should take two seconds. Then they do it again tomorrow.

Lion Pike was built to end that cycle. We connect directly to your existing ERP and in-house systems and turn your data into clean, consistent, automated reports — delivered to the right people without anyone lifting a finger. No manual exports. No copy-paste. No inconsistency in how the numbers are calculated from one week to the next.

Most data tools are built by technologists who understand systems but have never run a shift, read a production report, or tried to explain a variance to a plant manager. At Lion Pike, we’re industry professionals first. We’ve spent close to two decades inside manufacturing operations — which means we know which data actually matters, where it hides in your ERP, and what decision makers need to see. We build for the end use, because we’ve lived it.

If your team is still spending hours extracting data that should already be in front of them, we should talk.
