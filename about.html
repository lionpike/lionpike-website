What we do

Your ERP knows more than you think.

Most manufacturing facilities are sitting on a goldmine of operational data. The problem isn’t the data — it’s the work required to get to it. Across decades of experience on the factory floor, we’ve seen the same pattern repeat: someone opens the ERP, pulls a table, pastes it into Excel, and spends two hours producing a report that should take two seconds. Then they do it again tomorrow.

Lion Pike exists to end that cycle.

We connect directly to your ERP, in-house systems, and the spreadsheets sitting on shared drives — and turn it all into clean, consistent, automated reporting. Clear dashboards and scheduled reports, delivered to the right people without anyone lifting a finger. Calculated the same way, every time.

Most data tools are built by technologists who understand systems but have never run a shift, read a production report, or tried to explain a variance to a plant manager. At Lion Pike, we’re industry professionals first. We’ve spent two decades inside manufacturing operations — which means we know which data actually matters, where it hides in your ERP, and what decision makers need to see.

We don’t just build dashboards. We help you understand your operation.
