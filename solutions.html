What we offer

Solutions built for manufacturing.

Every solution we build connects directly to your existing systems — no rip and replace, no lengthy IT projects. We work with what you have and make it deliver what you need.

01 — Reporting

Automated Reporting

Scheduled reports delivered directly to your team’s inbox — daily, weekly, or on whatever cadence your operation requires. Built from your ERP data, calculated consistently, and formatted for the people who actually use them.

  • Production attainment vs scheduled output
  • Month-to-date and rolling weekly sales
  • New order alerts as they hit your system
  • Any metric your ERP captures, on any schedule
02 — Dashboards

Live Operational Dashboards

Real-time visibility into what’s happening on the floor and in the business. Secure, browser-based dashboards your team can access from anywhere — no software to install, no spreadsheets to update.

  • Shift progress and production attainment
  • Sales performance by customer and region
  • Rolling period comparisons vs prior year
  • Filterable detail down to item and order level
03 — Integration

Data Integration

Most manufacturing businesses run on a combination of ERP data, spreadsheets on shared drives, and tribal knowledge. We connect those sources into a single, reliable pipeline so your reporting reflects the full picture — not just what the ERP shows.

  • ERP systems including Microsoft Business Central
  • Excel files and shared drive spreadsheets
  • Custom data sources and in-house databases
  • Microsoft 365 and email-based delivery
