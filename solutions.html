What we offer

Solutions built for manufacturing.

No rip and replace. No lengthy IT projects. We connect to your existing systems and make them deliver what you need — ERP-agnostic by design.

01
Reporting

The right numbers, in the right inbox, before anyone has to ask.

Scheduled reports built from your ERP data — calculated consistently, formatted for the people who use them, delivered automatically.

  • Hourly production attainment — pieces vs expected at that point in the shift
  • Month-to-date and rolling weekly sales by customer
  • New order alerts within minutes of hitting your system
02
Dashboards

Four views. Live data. Everything your operation needs to see.

Browser-based dashboards your team can access from anywhere — no software to install, no spreadsheets to maintain.

  • Production — shift attainment and RPO progress in real time
  • Sales — performance by customer, region, and item
  • Purchasing — open lines, cash due, and action flags
03
Planning

Know what to make before you run out.

Live weeks-of-supply by SKU, demand modelling from sales history, and inventory projections — so planners make decisions, not spreadsheets.

  • Weeks-of-supply per finished good with low stock flags
  • On-order visibility from open purchase and production orders
  • Planner inputs for safety stock, MOQ, and demand overrides
04
Integration

If your system has an API, we can connect to it.

ERP data, shared drive spreadsheets, custom databases — connected into a single reliable pipeline without disrupting what already works.

  • Microsoft Business Central and other ERP systems via API
  • Excel files, shared drives, and custom data sources
  • Microsoft 365 email delivery via Microsoft Graph
